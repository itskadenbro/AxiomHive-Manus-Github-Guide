# Ethical Framework: The Non-Harm Principle

The core of the AxiomHive system is the unwavering commitment to the principle that **non-harm is the ultimate measure of validity**.

## 1. Philosophical Foundation

AxiomHive operates on the belief that strategic intelligence must serve the greater good. All operational logic, analytical outputs, and decision-support recommendations are ethically grounded to prevent unintended consequences and promote safety.

## 2. Implementation of Ethics

| Aspect | Ethical Integration |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Model Training** | Datasets are screened for bias and harmful content prior to training. |
| **Operational Logic** | Hard-coded constraints prevent the system from recommending actions that violate humanitarian law. |
| **Human Oversight** | Mandatory "human-in-the-loop" protocols for all critical strategic decisions. |
| **Transparency** | AI outputs include confidence scores and reasoning paths for auditability. |

## 3. Commitment to Integrity

By prioritizing professional integrity and ethical validity over mere utility, AxiomHive ensures that its strategic insights are not only effective but also responsible. This commitment fosters trust with stakeholders and ensures the system remains a force for beneficial outcomes in complex environments.
