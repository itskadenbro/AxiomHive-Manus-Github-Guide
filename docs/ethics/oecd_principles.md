# OECD AI Principles: Institutional Implementation Guide

AxiomHive is committed to the **OECD AI Principles**, promoting innovative, trustworthy AI that respects human rights and democratic values.

## 1. Inclusive Growth and Sustainable Development

AxiomHive is designed to contribute to global stability and security, ensuring that its strategic insights support the greater good and sustainable operational outcomes.

## 2. Human-Centered Values and Fairness

| Principle | Implementation in AxiomHive |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Human Rights** | System logic is hard-coded to respect international human rights standards. |
| **Fairness** | Active bias management ensures equitable outcomes across all strategic domains. |
| **Non-Discrimination** | Algorithms are regularly audited to prevent the perpetuation of systemic biases. |

## 3. Transparency and Explainability

AxiomHive prioritizes transparency, providing human operators with clear, understandable information about the factors that influence AI-driven recommendations. This fosters trust and enables effective human oversight.

## 4. Robustness, Security, and Safety

The system is engineered to be resilient against adversarial attacks and system failures. Rigorous testing and validation protocols ensure that AxiomHive remains safe and secure in all operational contexts.

## 5. Accountability

Final accountability for the outputs and impacts of the AxiomHive system rests with the organization and the human commanders who authorize strategic actions. The system's immutable audit trail provides the necessary transparency for comprehensive accountability reviews.
