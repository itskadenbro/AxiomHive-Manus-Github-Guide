# Legal and Regulatory Framework

This document details the legal foundations and regulatory commitments of the AxiomHive system.

## 1. International Humanitarian Law (IHL)

AxiomHive is engineered to support compliance with International Humanitarian Law. The system's operational logic incorporates the core principles of IHL:
- **Distinction**: Algorithms are trained to distinguish between combatants and non-combatants.
- **Proportionality**: AI recommendations evaluate potential collateral damage against strategic gains.
- **Precaution**: System logic prioritizes non-kinetic solutions and minimized harm.

## 2. Data Protection and Privacy

### 2.1 GDPR Alignment
AxiomHive serves as a "Data Processor" and, in certain contexts, a "Data Controller." The system implements:
- **Article 25**: Data protection by design and by default.
- **Article 30**: Comprehensive records of processing activities.
- **Article 32**: State-of-the-art security of processing.

### 2.2 NIST AI RMF 1.0
The system's risk management strategy is fully aligned with NIST standards, focusing on the development of **Trustworthy AI** that is valid, reliable, safe, secure, resilient, accountable, transparent, explainable, and privacy-enhanced.

## 3. Liability and Accountability

AxiomHive is a decision-support system. Final accountability for all strategic actions remains with the human commander authorizing the action. The system provides a "black-box" audit trail (Immutable Logging) to support legal reviews and post-action analysis.

## 4. Intellectual Property

The AxiomHive architecture, proprietary algorithms, and documentation are protected under international copyright and patent laws. Use of the system is governed by the specific Master Service Agreement (MSA) between the provider and the client organization.
