# EU AI Act: High-Risk AI System Technical Documentation

In accordance with **Article 11 and Annex IV** of the **EU Artificial Intelligence Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689)**, this document serves as the technical documentation for the AxiomHive system, classified as a high-risk AI system.

## 1. System Description and Intended Purpose

AxiomHive is a Strategic Command and Intelligence System intended for use in high-stakes environments, including strategic command and intelligence analysis. It is designed to support decision-making while ensuring full adherence to the **Non-Harm Principle**.

## 2. Technical Specifications

| Specification | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Architecture** | Multi-layered (Ingestion, Processing, Storage, AI/ML, Presentation). |
| **Hardware** | Enterprise-grade compute with high-performance GPU acceleration. |
| **Software** | Python-based core with integration of OpenAI GPT-4o and custom modules. |
| **Data Governance** | Full lifecycle management with SHA-256 integrity hashing. |

## 3. Risk Management System

AxiomHive implements a continuous risk management system to identify and mitigate risks to health, safety, and fundamental rights. This includes:
- **Bias Detection**: Automated scanning of datasets and model outputs for discriminatory patterns.
- **Explainability**: Provision of reasoning paths and confidence scores for all AI recommendations.
- **Human Oversight**: Mandatory "Human-in-the-Loop" protocols for strategic approvals.

## 4. Data Protection and Cybersecurity

The system is engineered for "Privacy by Design" and "Privacy by Default," ensuring full compliance with **GDPR**. Cybersecurity measures include end-to-end encryption, robust access controls (ABAC/RBAC), and immutable audit logging.

## 5. Compliance and Monitoring

AxiomHive is subject to regular conformity assessments and post-market monitoring to ensure ongoing compliance with the EU AI Act and other global regulatory frameworks.
