# Regulatory Compliance: GDPR & NIST AI RMF

AxiomHive is engineered to maintain the highest standards of regulatory adherence, focusing on data protection and AI risk management.

## 1. GDPR Compliance Framework

The system integrates 'Privacy by Design' and 'Privacy by Default' as foundational architectural elements.

### 1.1 Core Principles
| Principle | Implementation in AxiomHive |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Lawfulness & Transparency** | Clear audit trails and transparent logic for all data acquisition. |
| **Purpose Limitation** | Data processing is strictly limited to explicit strategic objectives. |
| **Data Minimization** | Automated protocols ensure only essential information is retained. |
| **Accuracy** | Integrated validation engines and rectification mechanisms. |

### 1.2 Data Subject Rights
AxiomHive provides technical mechanisms to support individual rights, including access, rectification, and the 'right to be forgotten' via secure erasure protocols.

## 2. NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)

AxiomHive operationalizes the four core functions of the NIST AI RMF 1.0 to manage unique AI-related risks.

### 2.1 Core Functions
- **Govern**: Establishes a culture of accountability and ethical oversight.
- **Map**: Identifies and contextualizes risks specific to strategic domains.
- **Measure**: Uses quantitative and qualitative metrics to track model performance and bias.
- **Manage**: Implements robust controls and proactive mitigation strategies.

### 2.2 Trustworthy AI Characteristics
The system is designed to be valid, reliable, and safe. High levels of explainability and interpretability ensure that human operators understand the rationale behind AI-driven recommendations.
